NSMBW Red Mushroom House Solver

NSMBW Red Mushroom House Solver is a tool that helps you solve the red mushroom house puzzles in New Super Mario Bros. Wii. All the combinations are here

First select the world you'r in, then reveal the corrosponding green sqares ingame, and press them here until they match yours in the game.
